Onion Soup

Description

Onion soup is a French dish that is made with caramelized onions and topped with bread and melted cheese. To make onion soup you will need to caramelize some onions then add ingredients to the pan to create a broth. After that you may top your dish with bread and cheese and soon enough you will have a bowl of onion soup.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cups sliced onions
  • 5 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons dry sherry
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper
  • 4 slices French bread
  • 4 slices provolone cheese
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese, diced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  1. Melt the butter in a 8 quart stock pot over medium heat. Add your onions and stir till they are translucent. Do not brown the onions.
  2. Add the beef broth, sherry, and thyme. Add salt and pepper and simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your oven's boiler.
  4. Ladle the soup into oven-safe serving bowls and place a piece of bread upon them. Put provolone, dices swiss cheese, and parmesan cheese on the bread.
  5. Place the bowls on a cookie sheet and broil them in the oven until the cheese bubbles (2-3 minutes).
