Description

To make chicken tortilla soup you need to first saute the onions and garlic then add spices like chili powder and oregano. After that you will need to add tomatoes, broth, and water. You may then add corn, hominy, chiles, beans, cilantro, and finally the chicken. All you have to do after that is cook the soup for a few more minutes and top the soup with any garnishes you would like and you're done!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can of condensed chicken broth
  • 1 1/4 cups of water
  • 2 teaspoons of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 can of black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 large boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup of whole corn kernels, cooked
  • 1 cup of white hominy
  • 1 can of chopped green chile peppers
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup of crushed tortilla chip, or to taste
  • 2 medium avocadoes, sliced, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup of shredded Monterey Jack cheese, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped green onions, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook till soft (5 minutes). Add crushed tomatoes, condensed broth, water, chili powder, and oregano then bring the soup to a boil. Proceed to reduce the heat and let the soup simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Add the black beans, cooked chicken, corn, hominy, chile, peppers, and cilantro. Simmer for another 10 minutes.
  3. Ladle the soup into serving bowls, and top with crushed tortilla chips, avocado slices, Monterey Jack cheese, and green onion or whatever else you'd like!
