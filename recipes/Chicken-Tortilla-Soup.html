Description

To make chicken tortilla soup you need to first saute the onions and garlic then add spices like chili powder and oregano. After that you will need to add tomatoes, broth, and water. You may then add corn, hominy, chiles, beans, cilantro, and finally the chicken. All you have to do after that is cook the soup for a few more minutes and top the soup with any garnishes you would like and you're done!
