Potato Soup

Description

To make potato soup you will need to bake bacon and cook the celery, onion, and potatoes in the bacon's grease. You will then need to add chicken stock to the celery, onion, and potatoes. Then you need to melt butter in another pan and add flour, heavy cream, tarragon, and cilantro before cooking. After you have cooked the mixture with flour and heavy cream you should add it to the original potato mixture. To finish you should blend half of the mixture then add what you blended back to the original mixture and before you know it you have a bowl of potato soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 ib. of bacon
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cups chicken stock, or enough to cover potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of dried tarragon
  • 3 teaspoons of chopped fresh cilantro
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
